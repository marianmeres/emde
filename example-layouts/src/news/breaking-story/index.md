# Open Source Funding Reaches Record High

Global investment in open-source software has reached an all-time high of $12 billion
in 2025, according to a new report from the Open Source Initiative.

The surge is driven by a combination of corporate backing from major tech companies
and innovative new funding models that allow individual contributors to earn
sustainable incomes from their work.

"We're seeing a fundamental shift in how the industry values open-source
contributions," said the report's lead author. "Companies are finally recognizing
that the software they depend on needs ongoing investment."

## Key findings

- Corporate open-source contributions grew 40% year-over-year
- Individual contributor income doubled through platforms like GitHub Sponsors
- Security-focused projects received the largest share of new funding
- European companies led in per-capita contributions
